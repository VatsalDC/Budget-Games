INSTRUCTIONS TO PLAY THE GAME
FOR SINGLE PLAYER MODE:
 Keyboard controls: Use Up/Down arrow keys OR W/S keys to move the paddle.

Mouse controls : Hover your mouse over the canvas and the paddle will follow it vertically.



FOR MULTIPLAYER:

Keyboard only: Move left paddle by W/S and right paddle with Up/Down arrow keys

Pong game is one of the first computer games thats ever created, this simple "tennis like" game features two paddles and a ball. You control the paddles and don't let the ball miss the paddle or the opponent gets a point. This game was originally developed by Allan Alcorn and released in 1972 by Atari Corporations. This game had hit the minds of people succesfully and became the first successful game in the history.
